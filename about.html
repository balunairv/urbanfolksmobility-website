The Spark

It began in college—with a daily struggle many commuters face. Long distances, multiple transfers, and limited information made every journey a challenge. Owning a vehicle wasn't an option, and public transport, though widespread, felt disjointed and outdated.

The Discovery

What started as casual conversations and shared frustrations soon became a deeper investigation. We began collecting data, mapping routes, and studying global transport systems. It became clear: the core infrastructure existed, but technology was missing. There was no bridge between commuters and the services meant to serve them.

The Inception

That's when Urbanfolks Mobility was born—with a vision to bridge this gap using smart technology and data-driven solutions. Our goal has remained simple:

Make public transport easy, efficient, and empowering—for everyone.

MISSION

To simplify and elevate urban mobility by leveraging cutting-edge technology and sustainable practices. We strive to make public transportation systems more inclusive, intuitive, and user-friendly—empowering commuters and shaping smarter cities.

VISION

A world where public transport is not just a necessity but a seamless experience—accessible, sustainable, and driven by innovation. We envision cities where people can move effortlessly, without the stress of poor connectivity or outdated systems.

URBANFOLKS

Revolutionizing urban mobility with technology, sustainability, and a human-first approach.
