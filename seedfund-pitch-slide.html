Why this matters

Each digital fare moves four things forward.

Beyond the bus, every transaction adds a rupee to India's digital rail, makes an MSME lendable, brings a commuter to public transit, and formalizes a town into the economy.

01 · Digital economy

₹54,000 cr/yr — moved into the digital rail.

India's largest un-digitised retail flow lives at bus stops. Every digital fare moves a rupee from informal cash into UPI — fueling the e-payments flywheel from a segment that's never been touched.

~₹14 cr/day Potential GMV — cash → digital
02 · Mobility & climate

Buses people actually choose.

When transit becomes predictable — live tracking, monthly passes, scan-and-go fares — commuters shift from cars and autos. Lower congestion. Lower emissions. Less PM2.5 in the air. A bus moves ~50× the passenger-km per litre that a car does.

~50× Passenger-km per litre vs a car
03 · MSME credit access

Invisible operators become lendable MSMEs.

Most small bus operators have no CIBIL, no GST trail, no online transaction data — invisible to banks, locked out of working capital and vehicle loans. allrides builds a verifiable financial profile from day one. Daily ops get easier; fleet expansion becomes financeable.

~1.8 lakh Operators outside underwriting today
04 · Bharat-first formalization

Not built for the top 1%

Stage carriage isn't Bangalore-Hyderabad intercity — it's the 5-bus operator running between two towns in UP. By building India-first for that operator, we formalize 1.8 lakh MSMEs, broaden the GST base, and create field-rep jobs in the Tier 2/3 cities that need them most.

~70% Of TAM in Tier 2/3-heavy states

Go-to-market

QR-first. Then POS. Then scale.

Most competitors lead with hardware. We lead with a 30-second yes — and earn the right to upgrade.

Phase 1 · M0–M3
01

QR-First Aggressive Onboarding

  • QR-only mode — zero hardware, zero CapEx for the operator
  • Geographic focus — 1–2 cities for route density
  • 5 cofounders + 1–2 field reps · direct sales + installs

POS Upsell + Revenue Validation

  • Convert 30–40% of QR users to POS for fraud control + deeper data
  • Bank EMI for POS — operator pays nothing upfront

Geographic Scale + Seed Raise

  • Use real cohort data to raise ₹3 cr Seed at M7–M8
  • Build the lending-data flywheel
