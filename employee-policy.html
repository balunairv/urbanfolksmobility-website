Employee Policy

Urbanfolks Mobility Private Limited

At Urbanfolks Mobility Private Limited, we are committed to creating a work environment that fosters innovation, collaboration, and professionalism while ensuring the well-being and growth of our employees. This policy outlines the key principles, guidelines, and expectations for all employees.

1. Code of Conduct

  • Employees are expected to maintain professionalism, integrity, and respect for colleagues, clients, and stakeholders at all times.
  • Harassment, discrimination, or any form of misconduct is strictly prohibited and will result in disciplinary action.
  • Confidentiality must be upheld to protect company data, customer information, and intellectual property.

2. Work Environment

  • Working Hours: The standard working hours are 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday to Friday. Flexible working arrangements may be discussed with supervisors.
  • Remote Work: Employees may work remotely based on project requirements, provided they maintain productivity and adhere to deadlines.
  • Office Facilities: Employees must use office resources responsibly and report any issues to the administration.

3. Employment Terms

  • Probation Period: New employees will undergo a probation period of six months, after which their performance will be reviewed.
  • Leaves:
    • Annual Leave: 18 days per year.
    • Sick Leave: 12 days per year with medical proof if requested.
    • Parental Leave: As per government regulations.
  • Performance Reviews: Conducted biannually to assess contributions and identify growth opportunities.

4. Public Transport Initiatives

Urbanfolks Mobility encourages employees to use public transport and contribute to our mission of promoting sustainable travel. Benefits include:

  • Subsidized public transport passes.
  • Recognition programs for employees who actively promote public transport.
  • Opportunities to volunteer in community outreach programs related to urban mobility.

5. Technology and Data Security

  • Employees must follow cybersecurity protocols to protect company systems and data.
  • Personal devices used for work must comply with IT guidelines and security standards.
  • Reporting data breaches or suspicious activities is mandatory.

6. Employee Development

  • Employees are encouraged to pursue relevant certifications and training programs. Reimbursement policies are applicable for approved courses.
  • Regular workshops and knowledge-sharing sessions will be organized to enhance skills and industry knowledge.

7. Health and Safety

  • The company ensures a safe and healthy work environment.
  • Emergency evacuation plans and safety protocols will be communicated to all employees.
  • Access to wellness programs and mental health support is available.

8. Grievance Redressal

  • Employees can report grievances or workplace concerns to the HR department.
  • All issues will be addressed promptly and confidentially.

9. Termination and Resignation

  • Employees must provide a minimum of 30 days' notice before resigning.
  • The company reserves the right to terminate employment with notice for valid reasons such as underperformance or policy violations.

Thank you for being part of Urbanfolks Mobility Private Limited. Together, we aim to revolutionize public transport and create a sustainable future. For further questions or details, contact hr@urbanfolksmobility.in.
