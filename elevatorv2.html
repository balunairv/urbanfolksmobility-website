A morning in Kochi

Meet Rithika.

She works at a tech company in Kochi.

Every morning, she takes public transport to office.

26 · Software engineer Daily commuter No car. By choice.
Tuesday · 7:52 am
She rushes out, hops on her usual bus, and realizes —

she forgot to take her wallet.

🪙
₹500
Reserve Bank of India
The only note in her wallet

Everywhere else — even the chai shop — accepts UPI. She didn't think twice.

On Bus 45 · 8:14 AM

She hands him ₹500. And then —

Where will I get change at 8 in the morning?!
Don't you know you have to carry change?
Sir… can I pay by UPI?
I don't have time to waste.
Please get down.
She is forced to step off — at a stop she doesn't recognise.
Stranded

Embarrassed.
Anxious.
Alone.

Standing at a stop she doesn't know — no idea when the next bus is coming.

Mole, bus will come every 5 minutes 👍

But the bus came after 45 minutes.

9:42 AM · 42 mins late

She walks in.
Boss walks over.

Meeting started 40 minutes ago. Again?

Her whole day — ruined.

Rithika · later that day
She kept thinking…
