Revenue build

Four streams, cohort-aware

Every cell sums across 36 cohorts. Ticket fees apply at life-month ≥ 5. Ads + ad-free pass introduced at L7 (after a 6-month rider awareness build) — pass adoption ramps L7–L11 (2%→10% of digital users at ₹49/mo); ad revenue covers the remaining users. Operator subscription kicks in at L ≥ 25, post-EMI. Digital commuters / day = new buses × 900 daily riders × 30% digital adoption = 270 commuters per bus per day.

Market sizing

India · private stage carriage
